Highlights of Spiti Valley Itinerary


Day 1: Arrive in Shimla and start your journey to Spiti Valley, stopping at Narkanda for scenic views and overnight stay.

Day 2: Continue the drive to Sangla Valley, known for its apple orchards and the stunning Kamru Fort, and immerse yourself in the beauty of Baspa River.

Day 3: Drive to Tabo and visit the ancient Tabo Monastery, known as the "Ajanta of the Himalayas," and explore the nearby caves and meditation chambers.

Day 4: Proceed to Kaza, the capital of Spiti Valley, and visit the Key Monastery, one of the oldest and largest monasteries in the region.

Day 5: Explore the picturesque villages of Langza, Hikkim, and Komic, and witness the unique culture and way of life of the local people.

Day 6: Visit the stunning Chandratal Lake, a high-altitude lake surrounded by mountains, and camp overnight amidst the serene surroundings.

Day 7: Conclude your Spiti Valley journey by driving back to Shimla, stopping at Kunzum Pass for panoramic views, and depart with unforgettable memories of the rugged beauty of the region.
